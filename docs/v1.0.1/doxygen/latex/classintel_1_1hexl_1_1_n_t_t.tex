\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Performs negacyclic forward and inverse number-\/theoretic transform (\mbox{\hyperlink{classintel_1_1hexl_1_1_n_t_t}{NTT}}), commonly used in RLWE cryptography. 

The number-\/theoretic transform (\mbox{\hyperlink{classintel_1_1hexl_1_1_n_t_t}{NTT}}) specializes the discrete Fourier transform (DFT) to the finite field $ \mathbb{Z}_p / (X^N + 1) $.
