\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Performs negacyclic forward and inverse number-\/theoretic transform (\mbox{\hyperlink{classintel_1_1hexl_1_1_n_t_t}{N\+TT}}), commonly used in R\+L\+WE cryptography. 

The number-\/theoretic transform (\mbox{\hyperlink{classintel_1_1hexl_1_1_n_t_t}{N\+TT}}) specializes the discrete Fourier transform (D\+FT) to the finite field $ \mathbb{Z}_q / (X^N + 1) $.
